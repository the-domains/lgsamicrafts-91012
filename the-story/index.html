The Story

Realm of Crafts

Two Scandinavian Trolls, designer brothers Dee & Mii, together with LG, the creator, they enjoy to bring all their ideas into being. Dee is giving birth to the dolls ,,LGsignD,, and Mii immersed himself in ,,LGsamiCrafts,,

We invite you to dive deep into the realm where they´re hanging out !

…just waiting for the tiiMe for millions of years…

This Amber had been sleeping patiently and undisturbed for millions of years on the sea bed. Not one of all previous wind storms had been able to wake it up. Until 6th of December ´77…

…when LG scared to death badly needed help to get rid of this monster, that pretended to be her friend. At that time, LG didn´t know, that this monster only was the old cleaning lady from her kindergarden. It could be a czar of Russia too and are they not dead?! Why she was asking higher power for helping her escape into the real realm, full of trolls and creatures. Her emotional storm became more and more stronger, and with some guidance from wind, so ice cold strong…

…that it woke the Amber. Scared to life, it floated trustful with waves on its long and dangerous journey, awake and ready to jump out of the sea into predestined land and patiently wait hiden in the seaweed. Not for the next millions of years, but only until…

…until next morning, where Keld from Hanstholm found it on his everyday walk at the west coast of Jytland. As soon as he looked throught it into the sun, LG woke up, hearing message was coming to her. From all sides she could hear…

…transform the form into spirit…

Keld gave it to his beloved sister Emmy when he returned home. And from that on, without taking any orders, all three was patiently following their nature, so simple like the Amber himself. Because more than one season will be repeated until…

… until Emmy, a wise old woman at that time, and LG met again. Yes, it felt for both exactly, like they just missed each other for long time. Their souls was sealed for eternity. Emmy gave shortly the Treasure to her soulmate, simple following her nature…

The first morning, as LG looked through the Amber into the sun, somebody standing beside her, with nothing than silence around him, was whispering…

…It´s tiiMe..Transform the form into spirit…It´s Mii…

That´s how Dee was created in Austrian Tirol. For some reason, like something still was missing, to be completed. But LG wasn´t worried at all. Nothing felt to be wrong, it felt only not to be whole.

In the TrollUniverse…following happened

It was necessary to bring Dee to LG first, cause Mii wasn´t ready to be created on tiiMe. While Mii was rebelling, Dee and LG was waiting patiently. In the TrollUniverse, Mii has created time of turmoil. He´s got enought to keep the traditions of being a classical Troll with traditional shoes, like his old-fashioned big brother Dee. Mii became a Rebel. If he should be born in LG´s Realm of Crafts, he definitely wants only “his own choice” shoes. So, that he can and will prove to everyone, that no shoes or traditions make a Troll to the “true” Troll. The whole TrollUniverse was schocked. They tried desperately holding on to their traditions. It´s difficult to break them, even if they doesn´t matter. But Mii was standing his ground. Trolls are intelligent, open and loyal creatures and they gave him freedom to make his own choice, becouse of tiiMe. That´s how Mii was comming to the world in southern Sweden.

It means, no more disguised cleaning ladies or czars, for the future. Finally, she´s got true helpers she can trust and they became infinite friends.

Dee & Mii are whispering…

…don´t forget follow your nature…
